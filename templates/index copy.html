Predicting MAFLD in T2DM Patients

Our innovative platform leverages machine learning to accurately predict the probability of Metabolic Associated Fatty Liver Disease (MAFLD) in patients with Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus (T2DM). By analyzing key lab test values, healthcare professionals can make more informed decisions, ultimately improving patient outcomes and streamlining the diagnostic process.

Utilizing a comprehensive dataset, our model considers various patient-specific factors to provide a tailored risk assessment. This personalized approach helps in early detection and timely intervention, reducing the burden of MAFLD and enhancing patient care efficiency.
